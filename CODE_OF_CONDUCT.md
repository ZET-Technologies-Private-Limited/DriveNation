# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and collaborate professionally.
- Give and receive feedback gracefully.
- Focus discussions on the project and technical outcomes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other communication channels.
